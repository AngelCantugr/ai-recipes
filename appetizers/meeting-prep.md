## Purpose
Prepare talking points and structure for technical discussions

## When to Use
- Design review meetings
- Planning sessions
- Technical deep-dives
- Architecture decision records (ADR) discussions

## Template

```
I'm preparing for a [type of meeting] about [topic]. Help me structure my talking points.

Meeting Context:
- Audience: [who will attend - engineers, PMs, leadership]
- Duration: [expected length]
- Goal: [what needs to be decided or discussed]

My position/proposal:
[What you're advocating for or presenting]

Key points I want to cover:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

Anticipated concerns or questions:
[What pushback do you expect?]

Please help me:
1. Structure these points in a logical flow
2. Identify gaps in my reasoning
3. Suggest how to address anticipated objections
4. Recommend what to prepare beforehand
```

## Tips
- Think about your audience's priorities and concerns
- Prepare data/evidence to support your points
- Have fallback positions ready
- Focus on "why" not just "what"
